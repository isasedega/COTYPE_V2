Aeonik is a versatile workhorse type family, crafted with mechanical precision by Mark Bloom and Joe Leadbeater. Conceived as a “neo-grotesque with a geometric skeleton,” it delivers rigidity in its strict perpendicular terminals – most evident in the letters ‘a, ‘t,’ ‘f,’ and ‘j.’ This is offset by the geometric roundness of letters like ‘c,’ ‘e,’ and ‘o,’. The balance between mechanical precision and timeless aesthetics has resonated with designers since its launch in 2021.

The family’s eight weights, ranging from the delicate Air to the commanding Black, are designed to tackle any creative challenge. For even greater control, variable font technology enables precise fine-tuning of weight. Italics are available for all weights (except Aeonik Arabic), and language support can be selected during checkout.

Aeonik’s OpenType features include discretionary ligatures, old-style and tabular numerals, fractions, case-sensitive punctuation, symbols, geometric shapes, circled numerals, and arrows. Its multiple stylistic sets across Latin, Greek, and Cyrillic offer alternate designs for characters such as ‘a,’ ‘t,’ and ‘j.’ Additional numerical alternates make the family fully customizable, ready to meet the demands of any project.

Our best-selling typeface continues to evolve. In 2025, Aeonik expanded its language support to Vietnamese, Arabic, Hebrew, and Thai.
